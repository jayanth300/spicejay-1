Spices

Parts of plants, like seeds, fruit, roots, bark, or buds, that are used to flavor, color, or preserve food.

Grains

The edible seeds of grass-like plants. Examples: Rice, wheat, corn (maize), barley, and oats

Pulses

The edible, dried seed of a legume plant. Examples: Lentils, chickpeas (chana), beans (like kidney beans or black beans), peas, and cowpeas.
